Proposed solution — GigCredit
A financial intelligence platform that turns UPI data into credit readiness
We take raw, unstructured UPI transactions and convert them into behavioral insights — generating a Loan Readiness Score, identifying financial gaps, and coaching workers toward approval.

Coaching + Report

Digital Loan Readiness Report

Real-time coaching

Transparent credit bridge
